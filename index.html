A CS/EE First-Year's Guide to UCLA Engineering

New to UCLA? Overwhelmed with starting college online? Check out the useful links below that will help you as you conquer (or survive) your first quarter as a Bruin.

The Classroom Essentials

These are your centers for classroom materials. Your professors and TA's will upload all their content to here.

    Make sure to have DuoMobile on your phone/tablet for simple authorization
  • CCLE : classroom materials uploaded here
  • MYUCLA : plan out your classes on your class planner, enroll in new classes, and see your final grades

Free Tutoring

These honor societies saved our asses. Check out each society's page for their schedule and available tutors. Usually, free tutors are able to help out Week 3-9. You can also ask for access to test banks and textbooks

  • HKN : the general engineering honors society
  • UPE : the computer science honors society
  • TBP : the electrical engineering honors society

Clubs!

These clubs are the most topical for CS/EE students. Most of these clubs offer a mentorship program for more guidance. Check out more clubs at the Enormous Activity Fair every fall!

  • ACM : Association for Computing Machinery
  • IEEE : Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers
  • WATT : Women Advancing Technology through Teamwork
  • SWE : Society of Women Engineers
  • AIRES : AI Robotics Ethics Society

Socials

With school being online now, it's essential to be connected digitally. You can find new friends, club events, cool new startup projects, and lots more through these platforms!

  • Facebook : discover and connect with new clubs and events here
  • Discord : chat with people in servers! most clubs have their own server
  • Messenger : messaging platform through Facebook

Engineering Mentorship

Looking for more advice? These are resources from UCLA Engineering

  • Academic Advising : book appointments with counselors
  • MentorSEAS : school of engineering group's of mentors
  • OASA : engineering Office of Academic and Student Affairs
  • CAPS : Counseling and Psychological Services
